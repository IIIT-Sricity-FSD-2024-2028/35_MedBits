# 35_MedBits
## Domain: HealthCare Administration (Non-Clinical)
## Problem Statement: Patient Appointment and Registration Management System
The system aims to streamline and digitalize the process of patient registration, appointment scheduling, and consultation management within a healthcare facility.  
This system provides a platform where:  
- Patients can register and book appointments efficiently.  
- Front desk staff can manage walk-ins, scheduling and billing accurately.  
- Doctors can access patient records, document consultations and manage follow-ups seamlessly.
## System Overview  :
The Patient Registration and Appointment Management System is a non-clinical healthcare information system developed to support the efficient registration of patients and management of appointments. The system enables patients to register their personal details and request appointments with doctors. Front-desk staff are responsible for managing patient records and scheduling appointments. Doctors can access and review their scheduled appointments. The system aims to streamline administrative operations, reduce paperwork, and improve overall efficiency in healthcare service delivery.
